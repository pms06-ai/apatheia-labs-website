Principles

What makes the methodology defensible.

These principles appear across every framework we studied and guide how the Phronesis engines capture, verify, and present findings.

Traceability

Every claim must map back to a source document, a specific quote, and a page reference.

Adversarial Verification

Findings are tested against alternative explanations to reduce confirmation bias.

Reliability Standards

Use repeatable methods, peer review thresholds, and confidence scoring.

Method Transparency

Readers should understand how a conclusion was reached without trusting the tool blindly.
